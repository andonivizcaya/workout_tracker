# Comprehensive Dumbbell Hypertrophy Program & Warm-Up

This program is designed for a setup consisting of two 4kg–40kg adjustable dumbbells, an adjustable bench, and two resistance bands (5kg and 10kg). It includes a universal warm-up routine and three specialized training blocks to fit your climbing and high-altitude hiking schedule.

---

##  SECTION 1: THE BANDED WARM-UP ROUTINE
*Perform 1–2 rounds of this circuit before every workout to mobilize joints and increase blood flow.*

### 1. Banded Circuit
*   **Band Dislocates / Pass-Throughs:** 10–12 reps (5kg band). Hold wide with straight arms, swing from hips over your head to your lower back.
*   **Band Pull-Aparts:** 15 reps (5kg band). Hold at shoulder width, pull apart until the band touches your chest. Squeeze shoulder blades.
*   **Banded Overhead Press:** 10–12 reps (5kg or 10kg band). Stand on the band, grab loops, and press overhead.
*   **Banded Good Mornings:** 12–15 reps (10kg band). Stand on loop, place upper loop around your neck, and hinge at the hips.
*   **Banded Squats:** 12–15 reps (10kg band). Stand on the band, hold top loops at shoulder height, and squat.

### 2. Dynamic Mobility
*   **World's Greatest Stretch:** 5 reps per side. Deep lunge with a torso rotation toward the front leg.
*   **Arm Circles:** 15 seconds forward, 15 seconds backward.

---

##  SECTION 2: THE HYPERTROPHY SPLITS (ULRULRR)

###  General Training Guidelines
*   **Progressive Overload:** Increase weight or reps whenever you successfully hit the upper rep target with clean form.
*   **Intensity:** Stop each working set 1–3 reps before absolute muscular failure (1–3 RIR).
*   **Rest:** Take 2–3 minutes between heavy compound sets, and 60–90 seconds for isolation work.

---

### VERSION 1: THE STANDARD SPLIT (Gym Only)
*Use this during standard weeks focused purely on lifting and recovery.*

#### Day 1: Upper Body 1 (Horizontal Focus)
*   **Incline Dumbbell Bench Press:** 3 sets × 6–8 reps
*   **Chest-Supported Dumbbell Row:** 3 sets × 6–8 reps (Bench at 30 degrees, face down)
*   **Flat Dumbbell Bench Press:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps
*   **Single-Arm Dumbbell Row:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps
*   **Dumbbell Lateral Raises:** 4 sets × 12–15 reps
*   **Incline Dumbbell Bicep Curls:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps

#### Day 2: Lower Body 1 (Anterior/Quad Focus)
*   **Dumbbell Goblet or Front Squat:** 3 sets × 8–10 reps (Dumbbells held at shoulders)
*   **Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift (RDL):** 3 sets × 8–10 reps
*   **Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squat:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps per leg
*   **Seated Dumbbell Calf Raises:** 4 sets × 15–20 reps (Dumbbells rested on knees)

#### Day 3: Rest Day

#### Day 4: Upper Body 2 (Vertical & Arm Focus)
*   **Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press:** 3 sets × 6–8 reps
*   **Dumbbell Pullovers:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps (Lying flat on bench, pulling from overhead)
*   **Dumbbell Floor Flyes:** 3 sets × 12–15 reps
*   **Dumbbell Rear Delt Flyes:** 4 sets × 12–15 reps
*   **Incline Dumbbell Tricep Extensions:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps
*   **Dumbbell Hammer Curls:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps

#### Day 5: Lower Body 2 (Posterior/Hamstring Focus)
*   **Heavy Dumbbell Romanian Deadlift (RDL):** 3 sets × 6–8 reps
*   **Dumbbell Walking Lunges:** 3 sets × 10 reps per leg
*   **Dumbbell Step-Ups:** 3 sets × 12 reps per leg (Use your flat bench as a box)
*   **Dumbbell Leg Curls:** 3 sets × 12–15 reps (Face down on flat bench, dumbbell between feet)

#### Days 6 & 7: Rest Days

---

### VERSION 2: THE CLIMBING MODIFICATION (Dropped Upper Day)
*Use this when replacing one Upper day with a rock climbing session. Climbing replaces your pulling volume.*

*   **Monday:** Upper Body (Push-Focused Mod)
*   **Tuesday:** Lower Body 1
*   **Wednesday:** Rest Day
*   **Thursday:** Rock Climbing Session (Replaces Upper 2)
*   **Friday:** Lower Body 2
*   **Saturday:** Rest Day
*   **Sunday:** Rest Day

#### Modified Upper Body Day
1.  **Incline Dumbbell Bench Press:** 4 sets × 6–8 reps
2.  **Flat Dumbbell Bench Press:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps
3.  **Seated Dumbbell Shoulder Press:** 3 sets × 8–10 reps
4.  **Light Chest-Supported Row:** 2 sets × 12–15 reps *(Warm-up volume only to avoid overfatiguing the back)*
5.  **Dumbbell Lateral Raises:** 4 sets × 12–15 reps
6.  **Overhead Dumbbell Tricep Extension:** 4 sets × 10–12 reps

---

### VERSION 3: THE HIGH-ALTITUDE HIKING MODIFICATION (Dropped Lower Day)
*Use this when training around a >5000m high-altitude hike to safeguard leg recovery.*

*   **Monday:** Upper Body 1
*   **Tuesday:** Lower Body (All-in-One Strength)
*   **Wednesday:** Rest Day
*   **Thursday:** Upper Body 2
*   **Friday:** High-Altitude Hike (Replaces Lower 2)
*   **Saturday:** Rest / Recovery Day
*   **Sunday:** Rest / Recovery Day

#### Modified Lower Body Day
1.  **Dumbbell Front Squats:** 4 sets × 6–8 reps *(Builds quad strength for steep descents)*
2.  **Dumbbell Romanian Deadlifts (RDLs):** 4 sets × 8–10 reps *(Strengthens hamstrings to protect knees)*
3.  **Dumbbell Bulgarian Split Squats:** 3 sets × 10–12 reps per leg *(Improves unilateral stability on uneven terrain)*
4.  **Seated Dumbbell Calf Raises:** 4 sets × 15–20 reps *(Builds ankle stability for climbing)*
